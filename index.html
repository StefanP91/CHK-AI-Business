Modules

Explore AI Before You Commit

At Creative Hub, we believe in informed decisions. That’s why we created the Pre-Academy
Experience – a series of free, invitation-only events to help you explore the world of AI and meet our team before enrolling.

  • AI Meetups - Network with industry pros and alumni at live events.
  • Webinars - Learn how AI is used in business and tech.
  • Free Courses - Try short lessons and explore our curriculum.
  • Meet the Lecturers - Connect with your future mentors and ask questions.

AI Foundations

Session 1: What is AI and Why Now?

  • Types of AI: Generative AI, machine learning, automation
  • Overview of business use cases: marketing, HR, finance, operations
  • ROI of AI adoption – success stories vs failures

Session 2: AI Landscape and Tools

  • Key platforms: Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, Google Vertex, OpenAI API, Zapier, Power Automate
  • Hands-on: Prompting with AI tools for email, research, documents
  • AI-readiness assessment for your organization

Business Functions & Process Automation

Session 3: AI in Key Business Functions

  • Sales & marketing: lead generation, content creation
  • Operations: inventory, scheduling, workflow automation
  • Customer service: chatbots, ticket triaging

Session 4: Data & Decision Making with AI

  • How AI uses data: structured vs unstructured
  • Dashboards, forecasting, and predictive models
  • Data privacy, governance, and security concerns

Strategy & Deployment Planning

Session 5: How to Choose AI Tools for Your Business

  • Build vs buy decision
  • Low-code/no-code tools vs custom solutions
  • Vendor evaluation: cost, integration, support

Session 6: Implementation Roadmap

  • Identify pilot use cases
  • AI integration plan: people, process, tech
  • Change management strategies (training, internal champions)

AI Readiness: Legal, Ethical, and Team Enablement

Session 7: Responsible AI in Organizations

  • Ethical AI principles
  • Bias, misinformation, and automation risks
  • Legal considerations: data protection, intellectual property

Session 8: Training Your Team for AI Adoption

  • Upskilling and digital literacy
  • Building AI awareness in non-technical teams
  • Internal communication and documentation

Project & Presentations

Session 9: AI Roadmap Project Work

  • Participants work on their “AI Business Blueprint”
  • Define a specific business process and how AI will be applied
  • Cost-benefit analysis and next steps

Session 10: Final Presentations & Peer Feedback

  • Present individual or team AI adoption plans
  • Group discussion: risks, ideas, feedback
  • Program wrap-up + certificate

Beyond the Academy: Join the AI Community

After completing the Academy, participants won’t just leave with knowledge — they’ll become part of an active AI community. Through exclusive meetups, Slack channels, and tech events, they'll:

  • Exchange ideas with other AI practitioners
  • Stay updated on the latest trends and tools
  • Build professional connections across industries
  • Get support as they implement AI in real-world settings

This is where learning becomes leadership — empowering your team to grow, contribute, and stay ahead in the ever-evolving AI ecosystem.
